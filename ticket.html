Combined Joint Special Operations Task Force 315 'Task Force Green'
Bound by Blood
Task Force Green is a mixed Tier 1/Tier 2 level, multinational special operations task force assigned to the United Nations and North Atlantic Treaty Organisation. Made up of multiple note-worthy forces from across the UN and NATO the task force is suitable for a never-ending list of tasks, including Direct Action, Hostage Rescue, Unconventional Warfare, Forward Air Control/Combat Control, Site Exploitation, Support and Assistance, Long Range, Special, and Covert Reconnaissance, Clandestine Surveillance and Reconnaissance, Close Protection, Combat Explosive Ordinance Disposal, and Chemical Biological Radiological, and Nuclear Threat Disposal.
