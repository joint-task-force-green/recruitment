Joint Task Force Green

Joint Task Force Green is an elite multi-national Combined Special Operations Joint Task Force between a number of UN involved nations. The role of the group in terms of Lore Roleplay is that while on standby for combat deployments (Whether it be a terror involved hostage situation in London, or a combat EOD and security force assistance deployment to Mali).
Members of the unit are recruited by members themselves, and are to undertake a selection and assessment scenario. Applicants are expected to be of a mature level-headed nature with adequate self control. Military knowledge is preferable however you will be given assistance in setting up your OC and will receive training should you pass the A&S course and enter the unit, which currently caps at 60.

Phase 1 - Aptitude

Aptitude and Assessment consists of a briefing course, in which you will learn the basics about the course and may ask any questions and then of the FTX. The Field Training Exercise is a SERE exercise designed to test candidates aptitude and ability.

Continuation Training

Continuation Training is the employment training of operators who pass the aptitude and assessment, they are badged into JTFG on probation and are put through multiple forms of different training they will need to function as an operator.

Who are we?

Joint Task Force Green (Officially Combined Joint Special Operations Task Force 315), is a multi-tiered premier special missions unit (SMU) of the United Nations and North Atlantic Treaty Organisation
and all of it's countries. It is made up of elements from all UN and NATO member nations.
